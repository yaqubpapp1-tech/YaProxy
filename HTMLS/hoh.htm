    House of Hazards

    What is the House of Hazards game?

    House of Hazards is a fun obstacle course game in which each player takes turns to complete tasks around the house whilst avoiding various hazards including falling lights, swinging cabinets, bursting taps, etc. Other players can activate these hazards in real time to prevent the current player from accomplishing their tasks.

    How to Play

    • Player 1:
      • A/D to Move
      • W to Jump
      • S to Crouch or Activate hazard
    • Player 2:
      • J/L to Move
      • I to Jump
      • K to Crouch or Activate hazard
